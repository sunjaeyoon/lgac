Who we are

Welcome to the Laguardia Aquatic Twisters Swim Club, recognized as one of the most elite competitive swim programs in the nation. We have consistently been the top performing New York City Metropolitan Area swim club for the past 20 years.
Since 1979, LGAC Twisters has provided a competitive swimming experience for thousands of children throughout Queens. Twisters is consistently one of the top 20 teams in USA Swimming's Virtual Club Championships. Our teams have won Junior National, Sectional, Senior Metropolitan, and Junior Olympic titles.
